Spend your holidays in nature !

Open View Place is a project created for you, to enjoy the nature. It's surface is 0.5 hectares, surrounded by mixed forests and a panoramic view over the Vaduri Lake and the mountains around. It is a location for hikes to Ceahlău Mountain, Bicaz Dam, Bicaz Canyon, Red Lake, Piatra Neamț City.
